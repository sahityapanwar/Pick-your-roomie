How Room Allotment Works

1
Form a trio
Invite a roommate to form a duo. From there, either invite a third student yourselves — your partner must approve before it's sent — or let a student request to join, which both of you need to accept.
2
Lock it in before 1 August
Once your trio is complete, the original two roommates can lock it in to finalize your lineup. Unlocking it again needs all three of you to agree.
3
Go to college and get your room.
There's no hostel office or warden assigning rooms — you and your trio simply move into the hostel and choose your favorite available room yourselves, on a first-come, first-served basis.
